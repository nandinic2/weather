New York

Women's International Forum (WIF)

About:

The Women’s International Forum is delighted to invite its members to the screening of the film « The man who mends women » on 18 March at 6.30 pm at Cinema Village, 22 East 12th Street. WIF is collaborating with the Socially Relevant Film Festival to bring this movie exclusively to WIF members. The movie chronicles the life and work of the 2018 Nobel Price gynecologist and human rights activist Dr Denis Mukwege and his efforts to heal the women who have been raped in the conflict in the Democratic Republic of Congo.
